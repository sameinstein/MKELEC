About Us

MK Electricals Limited is a limited Liability company registered and incorporated in Kampala, Uganda on the 28th day of April 2000 to particularly give specialized and reliable service in General engineering works.
The company has been in the field of engineering for over 21 years now.

The company was formed by dynamic professionals with vast engineering experience totaling to over 25 years whose expertise in project, contract planning, Implementation, management and administration is combined with expertise in a number of sectors.
Our root objective is to be reliable and quick service provider and encounter challenges that have emerged due to the globalization and liberation of the economy.
